Who We Are

Prospect Marketers is an independent contract marketing and consultation firm. We provide institutional marketing consultation services, institutional distribution and client relationship management solutions to asset managers seeking to build, expand or enhance their institutional marketing and client service efforts.

What We Offer

Experience

Prospect Marketers brings a wealth of hands on marketing and client management skills to money management clients, backed by 20 years of institutional sales and marketing experience

Demonstrated Production

Over $2 billion in assets raised for money management firms in separate accounts, institutional comingled vehicles, limited partnerships and defined contribution investment only(DCIO) mandates

Product Knowledge

US Small, Mid and Large Cap Equities, International and ADRs; US Fixed, Global, International and High Yield Fixed Income; ESG /SRI strategies, Alternatives, DCIO, OCIO
